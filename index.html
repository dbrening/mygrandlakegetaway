The Cabin

A Cozy Retreat in the Heart of the Rockies

Nestled near the shores of Grand Lake — Colorado's largest natural lake — this charming mountain cabin puts you steps away from world-class hiking, skiing, boating, and stargazing.

The western entrance to Rocky Mountain National Park is just minutes away, giving you access to over 350 miles of trails, abundant wildlife, and breathtaking alpine scenery without the crowds of the eastern side.

The Area

Explore Grand Lake & RMNP

⛰ Summer Adventures

  • Hike 350+ miles of RMNP trails
  • Kayak & paddleboard on Grand Lake
  • Fish for trout on Shadow Mountain Lake
  • Mountain bike on Monarch Lake Trail
  • Wildlife watching — elk, moose, bear
  • Trail Head Golf Course (5 min away)

☃ Winter Adventures

  • Ski & snowboard at SolVista Basin
  • Snowshoe through Rocky Mountain NP
  • Cross-country ski on groomed trails
  • Ice fishing on Grand Lake
  • Snowmobile on 300+ miles of trails
  • Cozy après-ski in Grand Lake Village
