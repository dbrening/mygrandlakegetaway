The Cabin

A Cozy Retreat in the Heart of the Rockies

Nestled near the shores of Grand Lake — Colorado's largest natural lake — this charming mountain cabin puts you steps away from world-class hiking, skiing, boating, and stargazing. Whether you're here to summit peaks or simply unwind on the deck, this is your home base for unforgettable Rocky Mountain memories.

The western entrance to Rocky Mountain National Park is just minutes away, giving you access to over 350 miles of trails, abundant wildlife, and breathtaking alpine scenery without the crowds of the eastern side.
